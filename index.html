About

My main research interests lie at the interface of representation theory and machine learning. Specific topics of interest include equivariant neural networks, model compression, and parameter space symmetries. My background is in geometric representation theory. For my papers and preprints, see the research page.

I completed a PhD at the University of Texas at Austin in 2016 under the guidance of David Ben-Zvi. I was subsequently a postdoc in the Hausel group at IST Austria, and in the research group of Rami Aizenbud at the Weizmann Institute of Science.
