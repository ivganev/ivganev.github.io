About

My research area is geometric representation theory. Specific topics of interest include the wonderful compactification, D-modules, quantum groups, quiver varieties, Azumaya algebras, and Deligne-Lusztig theory.

My papers and preprints are listed on my research page, and my CV is available here.

I completed my PhD at the University of Texas at Austin in 2016 under the guidance of David Ben-Zvi. I was a postdoc in the Hausel group at IST Austria from 2016 to 2019.
